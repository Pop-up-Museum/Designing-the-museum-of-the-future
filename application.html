consents

Pre-enrolment is free of charge and not binding.

The Summer School Designing the Museum of the Future will be activated once the minimum number of participants required is reached. On 17 August all pre-registered applicants will receive an email communication about the activation of the course; if confirmed, instructions will follow for paying the fee and finalising enrolment, which will take place on site at ACME Academy Milan.

Should the minimum number not be reached, the organisers reserve the right to reschedule the Designing the Museum of the Future project, having first notified all pre-registered applicants.
