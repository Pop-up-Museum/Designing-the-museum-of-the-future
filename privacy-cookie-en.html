Privacy and cookies

who processes your data

Data controller: Leonardo da Vinci S.r.l., Via dei Cattaneo 14, 28100 Novara (NO), Italy — VAT/Tax code 01044700035.

what data we collect

Your data is collected only at the point of application, through the application form: name, contact details, personal information and whatever you choose to share in your application. We do not record access logs, IP addresses, browsing history, or other data about your use of the site.

cookies

This site does not use tracking or profiling cookies. No third-party cookies or traffic-analysis tools (such as Google Analytics or similar) are set. The pages don't ask for cookie consent because, quite simply, they don't install any.

Purpose: assessment of applications, enrolment management and, if admitted, contractual and invoicing obligations. Legal basis: consent and pre-contractual measures.

We retain your data for as long as needed to assess the application and, if admitted, for the administrative and tax obligations required by law.

your rights

You have the right to access, rectify and erase your data, and to object to processing at any time, by writing to comunicazione@acmemilano.it.
